What is an online IQ test?

An online IQ test is a quick way to assess intelligence level. Our free IQ test consists of 7 logic tasks that help determine your approximate IQ range. Taking the test takes only 2-3 minutes, requires no registration and is completely free.

How does the quick IQ test work?

The quick IQ test includes various types of tasks: number sequences, analogies, logic problems and simple math. After completion, you will receive an assessment of your IQ with a range indication (for example, "approximately 120, but between 100 and 160"). This will help you understand your approximate intelligence level.

For a more accurate IQ assessment, we recommend taking extended IQ tests, which contain 40-80+ questions and take 15-60 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions about IQ Test

How long does it take to complete the IQ test?
